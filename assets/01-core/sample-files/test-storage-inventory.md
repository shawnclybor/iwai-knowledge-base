## Auditor Notes

Key risks in priority order:

1. **HR Templates - OLD DO NOT USE** is still surfacing in search and being used. All 124 files need to be deleted or moved outside the shared drive.
2. **Template proliferation across 5 folders** — no single authoritative location. Canonical versions (HR Templates 2023 for general, Templates (Marcus) for construction) are not discoverable.
3. **State Law Summaries are outdated** but sit in a folder that implies authority. Confirmed unreliable in two separate sample documents.
4. **FMLA Policy - under 50** is legally incorrect and should be deleted immediately.
5. **Templates (Marcus) is a shadow drive** — construction knowledge is siloed from the rest of the team.
6. **Two onboarding folders** with inconsistent content — neither functions as an actual onboarding resource.
7. **17 undocumented client folders** in Other Clients/ — status unknown, require audit.
