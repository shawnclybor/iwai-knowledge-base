## Employment Classifications

### Full-Time Employees
Employees regularly scheduled to work 30 or more hours per week. Eligible for full benefits package after 90-day introductory period.

### Part-Time Employees
Employees regularly scheduled to work fewer than 30 hours per week. Eligible for limited benefits as outlined in the Benefits section.

### Tipped Employees
Employees who customarily receive more than $20/month in tips. Subject to tip credit provisions under the Fair Labor Standards Act and North Carolina Wage and Hour Act.

- **Current NC tipped minimum wage:** $2.13/hour (federal tipped minimum)
- **Employer must ensure total compensation meets:** $7.25/hour (federal minimum wage)
- **Tip pooling:** Permitted among customarily tipped employees. Back-of-house employees may participate in tip pool under current DOL guidance. [NOTE: Verify current DOL rule — this changed in 2024]

### Seasonal Employees
Employed for a defined period, typically aligned with peak business seasons. Not eligible for benefits unless otherwise specified.
