# New Consultant Onboarding Checklist

**Prepared by:** Truepoint HR Solutions
**Last Updated:** 2023
**Status:** Needs Update — Neither onboarding folder has a complete version

---

## Pre-Start (Before Day 1)

- [ ] Signed offer letter received and filed
- [ ] Background check completed and cleared
- [ ] IT account creation request submitted (email, system access)
- [ ] Laptop and equipment ordered
- [ ] Workspace / home office setup confirmed
- [ ] Welcome email sent with first-day logistics
- [ ] Client assignment list prepared (initial accounts)

---

## Day 1

### Morning

- [ ] Welcome meeting with [MANAGER NAME]
- [ ] Office tour / introduction to team (or virtual introductions)
- [ ] IT setup: laptop, email, phone, VPN
- [ ] HR paperwork:
  - [ ] I-9 (must complete Section 1 by end of Day 1)
  - [ ] W-4 (federal tax withholding)
  - [ ] NC-4 / SC-4 / VA-4 (state tax withholding — based on employee's work state)
  - [ ] Direct deposit enrollment
  - [ ] Benefits enrollment packet (30-day enrollment window)
  - [ ] Employee handbook acknowledgment

### Afternoon

- [ ] Systems training:
  - [ ] Google Workspace (Drive, Docs, Sheets, Calendar)
  - [ ] Client management system (if applicable)
  - [ ] Time tracking tool
  - [ ] Communication tools (Slack, Teams, etc.)
- [ ] Review client file organization:
  - [ ] Shared Drive structure
  - [ ] Where templates live (HR Templates 2023 for general, Templates (Marcus) for construction)
  - [ ] How to find client folders

---

## Week 1

- [ ] Shadow experienced consultant on 2–3 client calls
- [ ] Review 3 sample client files from different industries:
  - [ ] Restaurant client (tip pooling, DOL compliance)
  - [ ] Construction client (multi-state, OSHA, workers' comp)
  - [ ] Healthcare or tech client (HIPAA or PEO considerations)
- [ ] Complete compliance training modules:
  - [ ] Anti-harassment training
  - [ ] Data privacy and confidentiality
  - [ ] HIPAA basics (if handling healthcare clients)
- [ ] Review Truepoint service offerings and pricing
- [ ] Meet with each team member for 15-minute intro

---

## Week 2

- [ ] Begin working on first client assignment (with supervision)
- [ ] Review and understand:
  - [ ] How to conduct a client needs assessment
  - [ ] How to customize a handbook from a template
  - [ ] How to identify compliance gaps for a client
- [ ] Complete first deliverable draft and submit for review
- [ ] Schedule weekly 1:1 with manager (ongoing)

---

## Month 1

- [ ] Complete all required training modules
- [ ] Handle at least 2 client interactions independently
- [ ] File organization: confirm access to all necessary Drive folders
- [ ] Benefits enrollment completed (30-day deadline)
- [ ] 30-day check-in with manager:
  - [ ] How is the workload?
  - [ ] Any gaps in training or access?
  - [ ] Client feedback so far?

---

## Month 2–3

- [ ] Manage 3+ client accounts with minimal supervision
- [ ] Complete first compliance audit independently
- [ ] Contribute to at least one template update or improvement
- [ ] 90-day review with manager:
  - [ ] Performance against expectations
  - [ ] Areas for development
  - [ ] Client satisfaction scores
